# Lead Generation Systems

We build automated lead generation pipelines that source, qualify, research, and engage prospects at a scale no human team can match. These systems run continuously, delivering warm leads into your sales process while your team focuses on closing.

<Screenshot src="/images/architecture/lead-gen-pipeline.svg" alt="Lead generation pipeline with five stages from reply detection to send" caption="Five-stage pipeline: detect, research, strategize, build, send - all orchestrated by AI." />

## The pipeline

Every lead generation system we build follows a proven five-stage architecture.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reply detection">
    The system monitors your outbound channels - email and LinkedIn - for replies. When a prospect responds, the system classifies their intent and routes them into the pipeline automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Company research">
    AI agents gather detailed intelligence on each prospect's company - industry, size, tech stack, recent news, competitive landscape, and key business challenges. This research feeds directly into the strategy stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI strategy generation">
    A multi-stage analysis pipeline evaluates the prospect's business, calculates potential ROI, and generates a tailored engagement strategy. Every recommendation is specific to that company, not a generic template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deck generation">
    The system automatically generates a customized proposal deck for each qualified prospect, pulling in the research findings, ROI projections, and recommended approach. Ready for your team to review and send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Your team reviews the generated materials and sends the response. The system handles the research, strategy, and preparation - your people handle the human conversation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What this means for your business

Manual prospecting typically limits a salesperson to 20-50 personalized outreach touches per day. Our systems increase prospecting capacity by roughly 1,000x while maintaining personalization that feels handcrafted.

The result is a predictable, measurable pipeline your team can operate independently after training. You see exactly where every lead is in the process, what research has been gathered, and what strategy has been generated.

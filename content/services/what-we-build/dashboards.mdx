# Dashboards

We build custom dashboards that show you exactly what is happening in your business, updated in real time. No more digging through spreadsheets, switching between tools, or waiting for someone to compile a report.

## Built for the questions you actually ask

Every dashboard we build starts with one question: what do you need to know, and when do you need to know it?

We connect to your existing data sources and surface the metrics that matter to your decisions. Not vanity metrics. Not every number we can find. The specific information you need to run your business effectively.

**Common dashboard types:**

- **Sales pipeline:** Where every deal stands, conversion rates at each stage, revenue forecasts
- **Operations overview:** Task completion, bottleneck identification, team workload distribution
- **Financial summary:** Revenue, expenses, margins, and trends - pulled from your accounting tools
- **Project status:** Milestones, deadlines, blockers, and progress across active projects
- **Customer metrics:** Satisfaction scores, support volume, retention rates, engagement patterns

## How they work

Dashboards pull data directly from your existing systems - CRM, accounting software, project management tools, communication platforms. Updates happen automatically. When the underlying data changes, your dashboard reflects it.

Your team gets trained on reading the dashboard, understanding what the numbers mean, and knowing when to act. A dashboard nobody checks is as useless as a system nobody uses.

<Callout type="tip">
  Every dashboard is fully customizable. As your business evolves and your questions change, we update the dashboard to match.

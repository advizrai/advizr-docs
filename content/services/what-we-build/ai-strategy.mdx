# AI Strategy

Before we build anything, we figure out what is worth building. AI strategy is not a generic report about industry trends - it is a specific, prioritized plan for your business based on how your operations actually work.

## How we approach strategy

We start by mapping your business processes end to end. Every manual task, every handoff, every bottleneck. Then we identify which processes are the best candidates for AI - based on time spent, error rates, business impact, and implementation complexity.

The output is a prioritized roadmap with clear timelines and expected returns for each initiative. You know exactly what to build first, why, and what it will save you.

## What you get

- **Process mapping:** A complete picture of your current operations, including time and cost for each manual task
- **Opportunity scoring:** Each process ranked by ROI potential, implementation effort, and strategic value
- **Implementation roadmap:** A phased plan showing what to automate first, second, and third - with timelines
- **ROI projections:** Specific, measurable estimates for each initiative - hours saved, costs reduced, revenue gained
- **Technology assessment:** Which AI tools and approaches fit each opportunity, with clear reasoning

## Real outcomes

Our clients typically reclaim 25-50% of their manual hours after implementing the first phase of recommendations. Across all engagements, that translates to 1,500-5,000 hours reclaimed annually per organization.

Every recommendation ties back to measurable ROI. We do not suggest changes for the sake of modernization - we suggest changes that pay for themselves.

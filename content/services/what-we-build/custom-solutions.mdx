# Custom Solutions

When off-the-shelf tools do not fit your workflow, we build what you need from scratch. Custom AI agents, industry-specific applications, proprietary algorithms, specialized reporting - whatever your business requires.

## When custom makes sense

Not every problem has an existing solution. If your business operates in a way that standard tools were not designed for, or if your competitive advantage depends on doing something nobody else can replicate, a custom build is the right approach.

**Examples of what we build:**

- **Custom AI agents:** Purpose-built agents that handle specific business functions - research, analysis, communication, decision support
- **Industry-specific tools:** Applications designed for how your industry actually works, not how a generic vendor thinks it should work
- **Data pipelines:** Automated flows that collect, process, and route data from multiple sources into actionable formats
- **Internal platforms:** Tools your team uses daily that are built around your exact processes
- **Specialized reporting:** Analysis and visualization systems designed for your specific metrics and questions

## Our process

Every custom solution follows the same disciplined path:

1. **Discovery:** We map the problem, understand the constraints, and define success criteria
2. **Design:** Architecture and approach get specified before any code is written
3. **Build:** Sprint-based development with working deliverables every one to two weeks
4. **Test:** Thorough testing in conditions that match your real operations
5. **Deploy:** Production deployment with monitoring, documentation, and team training
6. **Iterate:** Ongoing refinement based on real usage and feedback

## Everything we build, you keep

Custom solutions built for your business are yours. You retain the code, the documentation, and the intellectual property. Active hosting and maintenance require a subscription, but the work product belongs to you.

Your team gets trained on how the system works, why it was built the way it was, and how to request changes. We do not build black boxes.

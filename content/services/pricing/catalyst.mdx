# Catalyst

Catalyst is the entry point. A focused build with essential training, designed for businesses that want to see what AI can do before committing to a full transformation.

## Investment

$3,000 to $5,000 CAD for the initial build, plus $500 to $1,000 CAD per month ongoing. This covers the system build, essential education, hosting, monitoring, and support.

## What is included

**Single high-impact system**
One targeted AI system built for your highest-priority need. This might be lead generation, workflow automation, a dashboard, or a custom solution - whatever delivers the most value fastest.

**Phases 1-2 of the curriculum**
- Phase 1 - AI Foundations: understanding what AI is and what it can do for your business
- Phase 2 - Prompt Engineering: learning to work with AI tools effectively using your real business scenarios

**Live sessions**
- Kickoff session with your assigned engineer
- Prompt Engineering workshop
- 2-3 coaching calls

**Async deliverables**
- Architecture overview of your system
- Loom walkthroughs showing how everything works
- Prompt templates for everyday use

## Designed as an on-ramp

Catalyst is not a small, standalone product. It is the first step toward Partnership. The system we build gives you immediate value. The education gives you enough understanding to see what else is possible. Most Catalyst clients see the results and decide to go deeper.

<Callout type="tip">
  Catalyst clients can upgrade to Acceleration or Partnership at any time. Your existing system and training carry forward - you pick up where you left off.
</Callout>

## What Catalyst does not include

Catalyst covers Phases 1-2 of the curriculum only. It does not include Workflow Mastery (Phase 3), Strategic Leadership (Phase 4), change management, team training for broader teams, strategy reviews, or monthly advisory. These are available when you move to Acceleration or Partnership.

## Who this is for

Businesses testing the waters. Organizations that want quick wins and tangible proof before committing to a larger engagement. Leaders who need to see results before making the case internally for a bigger investment.

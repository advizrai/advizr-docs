# Acceleration

Acceleration is for businesses that want meaningful AI transformation without the full advisory commitment of a Partnership. You get targeted system builds, structured education through three curriculum phases, and strategic coaching to make sure the investment pays off.

## Investment

$5,000 to $10,000 CAD for the initial build, plus $1,000 to $3,000 CAD per month ongoing. This covers multiple system builds, the expanded education program, hosting, monitoring, and priority support. Every dollar is backed by our [2x ROI guarantee](/docs/services/guarantees).

## What is included

**Targeted system builds**
Focused AI systems designed around your highest-priority opportunities. The build scope is more targeted than Partnership but still substantial - real production systems that deliver measurable results.

**Phases 1-3 of the curriculum**
- Phase 1 - AI Foundations: what AI can do for your business
- Phase 2 - Prompt Engineering: hands-on skill building with your real scenarios
- Phase 3 - Workflow Mastery: understanding and operating your deployed systems

**Live sessions**
- Kickoff session with your assigned engineer
- Prompt Engineering workshop
- Workflow Workshop
- 4 coaching calls
- Team Training for broader team members
- Strategy Review session

**Async deliverables**
- Loom walkthroughs of your systems
- Operations runbook
- Prompt templates

## What makes Acceleration different

Acceleration delivers the core of the transformation - real systems, real education, real results - without the strategic leadership and change management components of Partnership. It is ideal for organizations that have a clear sense of what they want to achieve and need the execution, not the strategic discovery.

Your team will be able to operate and maintain the systems we build. They will understand AI tools and use them effectively in their daily work. What Acceleration does not include is Phase 4 (Strategic Leadership), change management support, opportunity mapping, or monthly advisory.

<Callout type="info">
  You can upgrade from Acceleration to Partnership at any time. Many clients start here and move to Partnership once they see the results and want to go deeper.
</Callout>

## Who this is for

Businesses with specific, defined AI opportunities that want to move fast. Organizations that know what they want to build and need a team that can deliver it while training their people along the way.

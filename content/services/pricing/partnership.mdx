# Transformation Partnership

The Transformation Partnership is Advizr's flagship engagement. It is designed for businesses ready for comprehensive AI transformation - not just a project, but a fundamental shift in how your organization operates.

From $10,000 CAD for the initial build, plus $3,000 to $5,000 CAD per month ongoing. Every dollar is backed by our [2x ROI guarantee](/docs/services/guarantees).

## The first 90 days

**Month 1:** Kickoff, process mapping, first sprint builds, education sessions begin. Your team starts learning while the systems take shape.

**Month 2:** Core systems go live. Education deepens into workflow mastery and strategic thinking. Your team starts operating the deployed systems independently.

**Month 3:** Full deployment complete. Strategic leadership training begins. Opportunity mapping identifies the next wave of improvements. ROI is measured and reported.

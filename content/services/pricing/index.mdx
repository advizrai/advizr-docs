# Pricing

Advizr offers three engagement tiers. All three include both Build and Educate — these are never separate. Pricing scales with the scope of systems built and the depth of training provided. All prices are in CAD.

## The numbers

| Tier | Initial build | Monthly retainer | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Catalyst](/docs/services/pricing/catalyst) | $3,000–5,000 | $500–1,000 | A focused first build — one core workflow automated |
| [Acceleration](/docs/services/pricing/acceleration) | $5,000–10,000 | $1,000–3,000 | Multiple workflows, integrations, ongoing optimization |
| [Partnership](/docs/services/pricing/partnership) | $10,000+ | $3,000–5,000 | Full transformation with the complete education arc |

The initial build covers discovery, design, and deployment of your first systems. The monthly retainer covers ongoing builds, education sessions, advisory, and support. Exact pricing within each range depends on scope and the size of your organization — you'll have a fixed number in writing before anything starts.

## Our pricing philosophy

As AI tools become easier to build, the pure build model gets commoditized. Anyone will eventually be able to wire together automations. The sustainable value is in knowing what to build, why, and how to make an organization actually adopt it.

This is why every tier includes education. The build anchors the relationship. The education drives the ongoing value. Together, they deliver transformation that compounds over time.

## What every tier includes

- **Build and Educate combined** — systems and training delivered simultaneously
- **2x ROI guarantee** — payback in less than 90 days, or we work for free
- **Cancel anytime** — no long-term contracts, no lock-in periods
- **Adoption guarantee** — we ensure your team actually uses what we build
- **Dedicated engineer** — a real person assigned to your engagement
- **Documentation** — everything built is documented and explained

No hidden fees. No surprise charges. The price you agree to is the price you pay.

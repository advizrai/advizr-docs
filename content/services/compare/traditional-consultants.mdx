# Advizr vs traditional consultants

The AI consulting industry mostly sells analysis. We sell working systems with the analysis built in. Here's the concrete difference.

## Deliverables

| | Traditional consulting | Advizr |
|---|---|---|
| Primary deliverable | Strategy deck + recommendations | Production systems your business runs on |
| Who implements | You (or a second engagement) | We do, weeks 1–8 |
| Team capability | Workshop, maybe | Structured education program, every tier |
| Pricing | Day rates, open-ended scope | Fixed build price + flat monthly, [published](/docs/services/pricing) |
| Accountability | Effort | [2x ROI guarantee, measured](/docs/services/roi-methodology) |
| Documentation | The deck | Living docs — this site is the public version |

## The structural difference

A consultancy's incentive is to extend the engagement; the deliverable is advice, and advice is never finished. Our retainer only survives if the systems keep producing measurable value — which is why we publish pricing, document our methods publicly, and guarantee the outcome. The incentives point the same direction yours do.

## When a traditional firm fits better

Enterprise-wide programs across thousands of seats, regulatory-heavy transformation in banking or healthcare, or situations where you need a brand name on the board deck. We're built for small and mid-sized businesses that want working systems and a capable team more than a binder.

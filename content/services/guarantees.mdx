# Guarantees

We back every engagement with three guarantees. These are not marketing claims - they are commitments we honor because our methodology makes them achievable.

## 2x ROI in less than 90 days

If the systems we build do not deliver at least 2x what you pay within 90 days, we work for free until they do. We are confident enough to guarantee it - you should be confident enough to try it.

This is not aspirational. Our average client ROI is 4.1x within the first 120 days. 100% of clients report meaningful time savings within 30 days. Typical results include 25-50% reduction in manual hours and 1,500-5,000 hours reclaimed annually.

We measure ROI in concrete terms - hours saved, leads generated, revenue influenced, costs eliminated. Not vague "efficiency gains."

## Cancel anytime

No long-term contracts. No lock-in periods. No cancellation fees.

We do not need contracts to retain clients - the value retains them. This guarantee signals confidence in what we deliver. If the engagement is not working, you can walk away. 92% of our clients choose to expand their scope instead.

**What happens if you cancel:**
- Advizr proprietary tools (LinkedIn automation, email systems): access ends - these are our products
- Custom workflows built for your business: you keep the code and pay a small hosting retainer
- You also lose ongoing advisory, strategic planning, and AI adoption guidance in a space that changes monthly

## Adoption guarantee

A system nobody uses is worthless. We guarantee your team will actually adopt what we build.

This is the education pillar in action. If your team is not using the systems as intended, we fix it - more training, a different approach, additional resources, whatever it takes. We do not consider the job done until adoption is real.

This is why Build and Educate are inseparable. The education ensures the build gets used. The build gives the education something concrete to teach.

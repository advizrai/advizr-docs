# Common questions before you sign

Straight answers to the questions every prospect asks. Anything not covered here — [book a call](https://cal.com/team/advizr/ai-strategy-call) and ask us directly.

<Details title="Am I locked into a contract?">
  No. Every tier is month-to-month after the initial build. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice — no termination fees, no lock-in periods. We keep clients by delivering value, not by contract.
</Details>

<Details title="Who owns what we build?">
  Custom workflows and systems built for your business are yours — you keep the code. If you cancel, you can self-host them or pay a small hosting retainer to keep them running on our infrastructure. Advizr proprietary tools (our LinkedIn automation and email systems) require an active subscription and remain ours.
</Details>

<Details title="What happens to my systems if I cancel?">
  Nothing breaks on day one. We hand over documentation, credentials, and the code for everything custom-built. You choose: self-host, have your own team take over (they'll have been trained), or keep a minimal hosting retainer with us.
</Details>

<Details title="Which AI vendors do you use?">
  Whatever fits the job — typically Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI models, orchestrated through our own deterministic execution layer (see the [DOE framework](/docs/architecture/doe-framework)). You're never locked to a single model vendor: the architecture is built so models can be swapped as the market moves.
</Details>

<Details title="Where does my data live, and who can see it?">
  Each client gets an isolated workspace and database — your data is never pooled with other clients'. Data residency, retention, and access details are documented in [data and privacy](/docs/platform/faq/data-and-privacy). We're a Canadian company and our agreements are governed by Canadian law.
</Details>

<Details title="How fast do we see value?">
  First working systems ship in weeks 1–3; full production by week 8. The [2x ROI guarantee](/docs/services/guarantees) is measured at day 90 using a [documented methodology](/docs/services/roi-methodology). All our clients to date have reported meaningful time savings within 30 days.
</Details>

<Details title="What if my team isn't technical?">
  That's our default assumption. The education program is designed for business owners and operators, not developers — if your team can write an email, they can learn this. The [Academy](/docs/academy) is the free, self-serve version of what we teach live.
</Details>

<Details title="Why not just hire someone or use Zapier?">
  Sometimes you should — and we'll tell you when. An in-house hire makes sense at sustained high volume; DIY tools make sense for simple two-step automations. Our lane is the middle: production-grade systems with judgment built in, plus a team that learns to own them, at a fraction of a hire's cost.
</Details>

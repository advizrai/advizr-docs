## Breez - Automated Lead Generation Platform

**Industry:** Professional services

**Challenge:** Manual lead generation was consuming hours of executive time daily. Outreach was inconsistent, follow-up was unreliable, and there was no systematic way to research prospects or generate tailored proposals. The sales pipeline was unpredictable.

**What we built:**
- Automated lead sourcing from multiple channels
- Multi-channel outreach system covering email and LinkedIn
- AI-powered company research that generates detailed intelligence on every prospect
- Strategy generation pipeline that produces tailored engagement plans per company
- Automated proposal deck creation with personalized ROI projections
- Reply detection and intelligent routing for incoming responses

The full five-stage pipeline runs end-to-end: detect replies, research the company, generate strategy, build the deck, and prepare for send.

**Education delivered:**
- Kickoff session covering the pipeline architecture and operation
- Prompt engineering training for refining AI-generated strategies
- Coaching calls focused on interpreting pipeline output and making send decisions
- Loom walkthroughs documenting every stage of the pipeline
- Operations runbook for day-to-day management

**Results:**
- Prospecting capacity increased roughly 1,000x compared to manual outreach
- Consistent, personalized outreach at scale - every prospect gets a tailored strategy
- Hours of daily manual research and proposal work eliminated
- Predictable, measurable pipeline with full visibility at every stage

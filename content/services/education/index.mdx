# Education Overview

Education is not an add-on. It is half of every Advizr engagement.

We build AI systems and train your team simultaneously. The education ensures your team understands what was built, why it was built that way, and how to use it effectively. Without education, you get software. With education, you get transformation.

## Why education is half the value

As AI tools get easier to build, anyone can wire together automations. The real value is in knowing what to build, understanding why it matters, and making your organization actually adopt it. A system nobody uses is worthless. A team that understands what is possible will find opportunities you never anticipated.

The more your team learns, the more they discover what AI can do for your business. Every capability we unlock reveals ten more they did not know existed. Education creates expansion, not churn.

<Callout type="tip">
  The build anchors the relationship. The education drives the ongoing value. Together, they create a compounding effect that grows over time.
</Callout>

## The 4-phase curriculum

Training progresses through four phases, each building on the last:

1. **Foundations** - What AI is, what it can do, and the transformation mindset
2. **Prompt Engineering** - How to communicate with AI effectively and get reliable results
3. **Workflow Mastery** - Understanding your automations, reading dashboards, identifying new opportunities
4. **Strategic Leadership** - Building your AI strategy, leading team adoption, change management

Which phases you receive depends on your [engagement tier](/docs/services/pricing). All tiers include at least Phases 1-2.

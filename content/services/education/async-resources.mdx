# Async Resources

Between live sessions, your team has access to on-demand learning materials tailored to your business. These are not generic AI courses. Every resource is built around your specific systems, workflows, and use cases.

## Resource types

**Loom walkthroughs**
Screen recordings that show exactly how your systems work. Step-by-step demonstrations your team can rewatch anytime. Updated whenever a system changes.

**Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)**
Written step-by-step documentation for your workflows. Each SOP covers what the process does, when to use it, how to run it, and what to do if something goes wrong.

**"How We Built X" guides**
Technical explanations of what was built and why. These guides help your team understand the architecture behind their systems, so they can make informed decisions about changes and improvements.

**Prompt templates**
Ready-to-use prompts for common business tasks - writing, research, analysis, communication. Pre-tested and optimized for your specific use cases.

**Operations runbook**
Your complete automation reference. Every system, every workflow, every integration - documented in one place. This is the manual for your AI operations.

**AI tool checklist**
Which tools to use for which tasks. A practical reference that helps your team choose the right AI tool for any given problem.

**Monthly AI brief**
A summary of what changed in AI this month and what it means for your business. New tools, new capabilities, new risks, and specific recommendations. Partnership tier only.

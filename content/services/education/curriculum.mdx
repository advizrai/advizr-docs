# 4-Phase Curriculum

Training progresses through four phases. Each phase builds on the previous one, taking your team from AI fundamentals to strategic leadership. The goal is not just technical competence - it is organizational capability.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1 - AI Foundations">
    What AI is, what it can do for your business, and the transformation mindset. This phase is for everyone on your team, regardless of technical background.

    You learn what AI tools are available, what kinds of problems they solve, and how to think about AI as a business capability rather than a technology project. This phase removes fear and hype, replacing them with practical understanding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 2 - Prompt Engineering">
    How to communicate with AI effectively. Writing prompts that produce reliable, useful results. Using different AI tools for different tasks. Practical exercises with your real business scenarios.

    Your team leaves this phase able to use AI tools independently for everyday work - writing, research, analysis, and communication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 3 - Workflow Mastery">
    Understanding the automations we built for you. Reading your dashboards. Identifying bottlenecks and opportunities. Measuring ROI on deployed systems. Proposing new automation candidates.

    This phase turns your team from users into operators. They understand not just how to use the systems, but how the systems work and how to spot new opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 4 - Strategic Leadership">
    Building your long-term AI strategy. Leading team adoption and managing change. Opportunity mapping across your organization. Evaluating new AI tools and approaches as they emerge.

    This phase is for leaders and champions. They become the internal drivers of AI adoption, capable of identifying opportunities and making the case for investment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

# What working with Advizr looks like

Advizr delivers AI transformation through two pillars: Build and Educate. We build custom AI systems for your business while training your team to understand, adopt, and extend them. We don't hand over a finished product and walk away, and we don't run a training session and hope it sticks — the two happen together, reinforcing each other for the life of the engagement.

## Why both pillars matter

As AI tools get easier to build, anyone can wire together automations. The value is in knowing what to build, why, and how to make your organization actually adopt it. A system nobody uses is worthless; a team that understands what's possible finds opportunities you never anticipated.

The build anchors the relationship — your business runs on the software we deploy. The education justifies the ongoing partnership — your team grows more capable every month and drives adoption from the inside.

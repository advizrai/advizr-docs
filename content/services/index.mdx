# Services Overview

Advizr delivers AI transformation through two pillars - Build and Educate. Every engagement combines both. We build custom AI systems for your business while simultaneously training your team to understand, adopt, and extend them.

This is not a typical consulting arrangement. We do not hand over a finished product and walk away. We do not run a training session and hope it sticks. Build and Educate happen at the same time, reinforcing each other throughout the engagement.

## Why both matter

As AI tools get easier to build, anyone can wire together automations. The value is in knowing what to build, why, and how to make your organization actually adopt it. A system nobody uses is worthless. A team that understands what is possible will find opportunities you never anticipated.

The build anchors the relationship - your business runs on the software we deploy. The education justifies the ongoing partnership - your team grows more capable every month, discovers new opportunities, and drives adoption from the inside.

## Results

Our clients see measurable returns quickly. Average ROI across engagements is 4.1x within the first 120 days. 92% of clients expand their scope after the initial engagement. 100% report meaningful time savings within 30 days.

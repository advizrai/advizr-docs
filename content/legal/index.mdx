## How these documents fit together

The [Terms of service](/docs/legal/terms-of-service) is the umbrella — the other documents plug into it. The [Privacy policy](/docs/legal/privacy-policy) covers personal information generally; [Data processing](/docs/legal/data-processing) adds the service-provider commitments for business data we hold on your behalf. [Software licensing](/docs/legal/software-licensing) settles ownership, [Acceptable use](/docs/legal/acceptable-use) sets the rules of the road, and [Security & compliance](/docs/legal/security) documents the controls behind all of it.

If you've signed a services agreement with us, that document governs wherever it differs from these published versions.

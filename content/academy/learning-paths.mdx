# Learning Paths

Three recommended routes through the Academy, based on your role. Pick the one that fits, or mix and match. There are no rules here, just suggestions.

## Business Owner Path

**For:** Founders, directors, and senior leaders who need the strategic picture without getting lost in the technical details.

**Estimated time:** 2-3 hours

**Courses:** Foundations + Strategic Leadership

**What you will learn:**
- What AI can and cannot do for your business
- How to identify the highest-value automation opportunities
- How to lead your team through the transition
- How to measure ROI and make the case for continued investment

You will come away with a clear plan for where AI fits in your business and the confidence to drive adoption from the top.

## Team Lead Path

**For:** Managers, department heads, and anyone responsible for getting a team to adopt new tools and ways of working.

**Estimated time:** 5-6 hours

**Courses:** All four, in order - Foundations, Prompt Engineering, Workflow Mastery, Strategic Leadership

**What you will learn:**
- The full picture, from basic concepts to advanced strategy
- How to write effective prompts and teach your team to do the same
- How to redesign workflows around AI capabilities
- How to handle resistance, measure progress, and keep momentum

This is the comprehensive path. You will understand both the hands-on skills and the leadership side.

## Technical Path

**For:** Operations managers, process designers, and anyone who will be hands-on with AI tools daily.

**Estimated time:** 3-4 hours

**Courses:** Prompt Engineering + Workflow Mastery

**What you will learn:**
- Advanced prompting techniques that get consistent, high-quality results
- How to build automated workflows using AI
- How to connect AI tools into your existing systems
- How to test, iterate, and improve your automations over time

This path skips the strategic overview and goes straight to the practical skills.

<Callout>
These paths are recommendations, not requirements. Every page in the Academy stands on its own. Explore whatever interests you.
</Callout>

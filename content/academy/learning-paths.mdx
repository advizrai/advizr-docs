# Learning paths

Three recommended routes through the Academy, based on your role. Each path is a checklist of specific lessons in the order to read them, with honest time estimates — these are short, dense lessons, not hour-long modules. Pick the path that fits, or mix and match.

## Business owner path

If you're a founder, director, or senior leader, you need the strategic picture without the hands-on detail. This path pairs the Foundations course with AI leadership and skips the practitioner material.

**Reading time:** about 21 minutes across 9 lessons, plus time to work through the getting-started checklist and the one-page strategy exercise.

- [ ] [What is AI?](/docs/academy/foundations/what-is-ai) — what LLMs do well and where they fail (2 min)
- [ ] [AI for your business](/docs/academy/foundations/ai-for-your-business) — the five-questions framework for finding opportunities (2 min)
- [ ] [The transformation mindset](/docs/academy/foundations/the-transformation-mindset) — why adoption is a people project first (2 min)
- [ ] [Getting started checklist](/docs/academy/foundations/getting-started-checklist) — concrete first steps and what to track (2 min)
- [ ] [Building your AI strategy](/docs/academy/leadership/ai-strategy) — map, score, and rank your processes into a one-page plan (2 min)
- [ ] [Team adoption](/docs/academy/leadership/team-adoption) — the adoption ladder and finding champions (3 min)
- [ ] [Change management](/docs/academy/leadership/change-management) — handling resistance and setting a training cadence (3 min)
- [ ] [Opportunity mapping](/docs/academy/leadership/opportunity-mapping) — expanding from your first win to annual targets (2 min)
- [ ] [Staying current](/docs/academy/leadership/staying-current) — what to follow, what to ignore (3 min)

You'll come away with a clear view of where AI fits in your business and a plan for driving adoption from the top.

## Team lead path

If you're a manager or department head responsible for getting a team working differently, take all four courses in order. This is the comprehensive path: hands-on skills plus the leadership side.

**Reading time:** about 44 minutes across 19 lessons, plus hands-on time for the prompt engineering exercises and the checklists.

- [ ] [AI foundations](/docs/academy/foundations) — all 4 lessons (8 min)
- [ ] [Prompt engineering](/docs/academy/prompt-engineering) — all 5 lessons, including the hands-on exercises (12 min reading)
- [ ] [Workflow mastery](/docs/academy/workflow-mastery) — all 5 lessons (11 min)
- [ ] [AI leadership](/docs/academy/leadership) — all 5 lessons (13 min)

You'll understand the full picture: writing effective prompts, teaching your team to do the same, redesigning workflows, and keeping momentum through resistance.

## Technical path

If you're an operations manager, process designer, or anyone hands-on with AI tools daily, skip the strategic overview and go straight to the practical skills.

**Reading time:** about 23 minutes across 10 lessons, plus hands-on time for the five exercises.

- [ ] [Prompt engineering basics](/docs/academy/prompt-engineering/basics) — the prompt-response loop (2 min)
- [ ] [Writing effective prompts](/docs/academy/prompt-engineering/effective-prompts) — the four-part structure with worked examples (2 min)
- [ ] [Advanced techniques](/docs/academy/prompt-engineering/advanced-techniques) — chain-of-thought, few-shot, system prompts, and more (3 min)
- [ ] [Tools and models](/docs/academy/prompt-engineering/tools-and-models) — which tool for which task (2 min)
- [ ] [Exercises](/docs/academy/prompt-engineering/exercises) — five real business scenarios to practise on (3 min)
- [ ] [Identifying opportunities](/docs/academy/workflow-mastery/identifying-opportunities) — the frequency-complexity matrix (2 min)
- [ ] [Automation basics](/docs/academy/workflow-mastery/automation-basics) — triggers, actions, and data flow (2 min)
- [ ] [Reading your dashboard](/docs/academy/workflow-mastery/reading-your-dashboard) — runs, statuses, and logs (2 min)
- [ ] [Optimizing workflows](/docs/academy/workflow-mastery/optimizing-workflows) — iteration, A/B testing, and error handling (2 min)
- [ ] [Measuring ROI](/docs/academy/workflow-mastery/measuring-roi) — baselines and the ROI formula (3 min)

You'll be able to get consistent, high-quality output from AI tools and design, monitor, and improve the automations built around them.

## After your path

Whichever path you take, two resources round it out:

- [Industry guides](/docs/academy/industry-guides) — sector-specific workflows for real estate, professional services, ecommerce, and construction (2 min each)
- [Glossary](/docs/academy/glossary) — plain-language definitions to keep beside you as you read

<Callout>
These paths are recommendations, not requirements. Every lesson stands on its own, so explore whatever interests you.

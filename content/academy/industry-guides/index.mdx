# Industry guides

Four standalone guides showing where AI fits in specific sectors. Each one covers the workflows AI handles well in that industry, a real before-and-after example, and a concrete place to start. Unlike the courses, these don't build on each other — read the one that matches your business and skip the rest.

## What you'll be able to do

After reading your industry's guide you'll be able to:

- Recognize the specific workflows in your sector — reporting, document handling, communication, research — that AI handles well today
- Set realistic expectations using before-and-after examples of time spent on those workflows
- Pick a sensible first automation for your business and run it alongside your current process before relying on it
- Keep the review steps that need your expertise human while automating the parts that don't

## Time and prerequisites

Each guide is about a 2 minute read — roughly 8 minutes for all four.

There are no prerequisites, though [AI foundations](/docs/academy/foundations) gives you the vocabulary these guides assume. If your industry isn't covered, the patterns still transfer: the [five-questions framework](/docs/academy/foundations/ai-for-your-business) works in any sector.

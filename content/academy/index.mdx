# Advizr Academy

Learn to transform your business with AI.

The Academy is a structured programme built for people who run businesses, lead teams, and make decisions. No coding required. No fluff. Just the knowledge you need to put AI to work.

## What you will learn

The Academy is organised into four courses, each building on the last:

1. **Foundations** - What AI actually is, where it fits in your business, and how to start thinking about transformation. The essential groundwork.
2. **Prompt Engineering** - How to communicate with AI tools effectively. The difference between a useless response and a brilliant one is almost always the prompt.
3. **Workflow Mastery** - How to connect AI into your existing processes, automate repetitive work, and build systems that run without you.
4. **Strategic Leadership** - How to lead AI adoption across your organisation, measure ROI, and build a team that embraces change.

## Who this is for

- **Business owners** who want to reduce costs, move faster, and stay competitive
- **Team leads** who need to bring their people along on the AI journey
- **Anyone curious** about what AI can actually do for real work

You do not need a technical background. If you can write an email, you can learn this.

## How to use the Academy

You have two options:

**Follow a learning path.** We have built three recommended paths based on your role. Each one tells you exactly which courses to take and in what order. Start with [Learning Paths](/docs/academy/learning-paths).

**Explore freely.** Every page stands on its own. If you already know the basics, skip ahead to whatever interests you.

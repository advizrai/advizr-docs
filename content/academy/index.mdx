# Advizr Academy

The Academy is structured AI education for people who run businesses, lead teams, and make decisions. Four courses take you from first principles to organization-wide strategy, supported by industry guides and a plain-language glossary. If you can write an email, you can learn this.

## What to expect

<Callout>
Most lessons are 5 to 10 minute reads, built around examples you can apply the same day. Work through a full course in an afternoon, or take one lesson at a time between meetings. Prefer to learn live? The same material is taught in live sessions as part of every Advizr engagement. See [Education services](/docs/services/education).
</Callout>

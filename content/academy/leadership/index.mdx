# AI leadership

Tools and prompts get you started; leadership makes adoption stick. This course covers the organizational side of AI: setting a strategy that fits on one page, moving your team up the adoption ladder, handling resistance honestly, expanding beyond your first win, and staying informed without drowning in hype.

## What you'll be able to do

By the end of this course you'll be able to:

- Build a one-page AI strategy: map your processes, score them on frequency, complexity, and impact, and sequence quick wins ahead of strategic bets
- Move your team up the five-stage adoption ladder using champions, templates, and small-dose training instead of mandates
- Respond to the most common objections — "I've done this for 20 years," "I don't trust it" — with honest answers and a communication plan
- Map cross-functional opportunities across departments, set annual automation targets, and run a monthly-quarterly-annual cadence for staying current

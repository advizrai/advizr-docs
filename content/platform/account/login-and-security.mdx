# Log in to your account

Your Advizr account is secured by our authentication provider. You sign in with your email and password at advizrclients.com. Your credentials are stored in a master authentication database, while your organization's business data lives in a separate, isolated database.

## Log in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to advizrclients.com">
    Use any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. On mobile, use your phone's browser and add the page to your home screen for quick access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    If your organization has multiple team members, each person signs in with their own credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign in">
    After authentication, you're redirected to your organization's dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should now see your Home screen, with the sidebar on the left: Home, Chat, Goals, Documents, Proposals, Learn, Book a Call, and Help.

## Reset your password

If you've forgotten your password, reset it from the login page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Forgot password">
    The link sits below the password field on the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type the email address associated with your account and click **Submit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    You'll receive an email with a password reset link. Click it to set a new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your new password">
    Choose a strong password and confirm it. You're redirected back to the login page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should now be able to sign in with your new password.

<Callout type="warning">
  Password reset links expire after one hour. If your link has expired, request a new one from the login page.
</Callout>

## How sessions work

When you log in, the platform creates a session token that lasts 24 hours. The session renews automatically while you're actively using the platform, so you're only prompted to log in again after extended periods of inactivity.

## How your account is secured

The platform uses several layers of security:

- **HTTPS encryption** — all traffic between your browser and the platform is encrypted in transit
- **Encrypted storage** — passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- **Isolated databases** — your login credentials are stored separately from your business data
- **Role-based access** — each team member sees only what their role allows (see [Roles and permissions](/docs/platform/account/roles-and-permissions))

<Details title="Why credentials and business data live in separate databases">
  The platform uses a dual-database architecture. A master authentication database handles logins, while each organization's business data — documents, conversations, goals, and activity — lives in its own isolated database. Even if you access multiple services, your business data stays in your organization's database, separate from every other client.
</Details>

## Browser support

The platform supports the latest two major versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported. For the best experience, keep your browser updated.

## If something goes wrong

- **You can't log in.** Double-check your email address, then use **Forgot password** to reset. If you still can't get in, email your FDE directly for a manual reset.
- **The reset email never arrived.** Check your spam folder, and confirm you entered the email address your account was created with.
- **The reset link doesn't work.** Links expire after one hour — request a fresh one from the login page.
- **You were logged out unexpectedly.** Your 24-hour session expired. Log in again. If it keeps happening, clear your browser cache.

Still stuck? Send your FDE a message in [Chat](/docs/platform/dashboard/chat) with your browser, a screenshot, and when it happened.

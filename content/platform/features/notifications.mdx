# Notifications

The platform keeps you informed about important events through two channels: in-app notifications and email digests. You control what you receive and how often, so you stay up to date without being overwhelmed.

## What triggers notifications

You receive notifications when something happens that may need your attention:

- **Proposals** - A new proposal is ready for review, or an existing proposal has been updated
- **Workflow completions** - A run has finished and results are available
- **Chat messages** - Your FDE or another team member has sent you a message
- **Team changes** - A new team member has accepted their invitation
- **Document processing** - A document you uploaded has finished processing
- **Goal updates** - Progress has been logged against one of your objectives

## In-app notifications

A notification badge appears on the bell icon in the top bar when new events arrive. Click the bell to see a list of recent notifications. Each notification links directly to the relevant section - click a proposal notification to jump to the proposal, click a run notification to see the results.

Notifications are grouped by type and sorted with the most recent at the top. You can mark notifications as read individually or mark all as read at once.

## Email digests

In addition to in-app notifications, the platform sends email summaries of activity. You can choose how often you receive these:

- **Daily** - A summary email each morning with everything that happened yesterday
- **Weekly** - A summary email each Monday with the previous week's activity
- **Off** - No email digests (you still get in-app notifications)

<Callout type="tip">
  If you only check the platform occasionally, set your email digest to daily. The summary email keeps you informed even when you are not logged in.
</Callout>

## Configuring your preferences

You can adjust notification settings from [Settings](/docs/platform/features/settings) or from the notification panel directly. Options include:

- Toggle email digests on or off
- Choose digest frequency (daily or weekly)
- Mute specific notification types (for example, turn off document processing alerts if you upload files frequently)

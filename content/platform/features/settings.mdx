# Settings

The Settings page is where you manage your account, preferences, and organisation configuration. What you see here depends on your role - Client Admins have access to organisation-level settings, while other roles see personal settings only.

## Profile

Your profile settings control how you appear on the platform:

- **Name** - Your display name, visible to your team and your Advizr FDE
- **Email** - The email address used for login and notifications
- **Avatar** - Upload a profile photo or use the default initials

Changes to your profile take effect immediately across the platform.

## Preferences

Preferences control how the platform looks and behaves for you:

- **Theme** - Choose between light and dark mode, or follow your system setting
- **Notification preferences** - Configure email digest frequency and mute specific notification types (see [Notifications](/docs/platform/features/notifications) for details)

<Callout type="info" title="Progressive disclosure">
  The platform supports a Simple/Advanced toggle. In Simple mode, the interface shows the core features - Chat, Goals, Documents, and the essentials. Advanced mode reveals additional sections like detailed analytics, workflow configuration, and technical settings. Most clients start in Simple mode and switch to Advanced as they get comfortable.
</Callout>

## Organisation settings (Client Admin only)

If you have the Client Admin role, you also see organisation-level settings:

- **Company information** - Your company name, industry, and description as shown in the platform
- **Team management** - Quick link to [Team Management](/docs/platform/features/team-management) for adding or removing members
- **Billing** - Quick link to [Billing](/docs/platform/account/billing) for invoices and payment methods
- **Connected integrations** - View and manage any third-party tools connected to your workspace

## Security settings

Security-related options are covered separately in [Login and Security](/docs/platform/account/login-and-security). From Settings you can access the password change form and review your active sessions.

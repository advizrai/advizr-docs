# Advizr Client Platform

The Advizr Client Platform is your workspace at advizrclients.com. It is where you manage your AI transformation engagement - from setting goals and uploading documents to chatting with AI and reviewing proposals from your team. Every client organisation gets an isolated dashboard with its own data, users, and configuration.

## What you can do

The sidebar gives you access to eight core areas:

- **Home** - Your daily overview. Action cards, progress checklist, and recent activity in one place.
- **Chat** - The primary way to interact with the platform. Ask questions, request deliverables, analyse documents, and get status updates.
- **Goals** - Set and track business objectives. Goals shape the recommendations and workflows your Advizr team builds for you.
- **Documents** - Upload business documents for AI analysis. SOPs, org charts, financials, and process maps give the platform context about your business.
- **Proposals** - Review transformation recommendations from your Advizr team. Approve, discuss, or request changes.
- **Learn** - Access educational content tailored to your engagement phase. Courses, guides, and case studies to build your AI confidence.
- **Book** - Schedule calls with your Fractional Digital Engineer (FDE). Strategy sessions, check-ins, training, and ad-hoc conversations.
- **Help** - Get support. Start with Chat for the fastest answer, or browse the FAQ.

## How your data is protected

Each client organisation gets a dedicated database. Your documents, conversations, and business data are never shared with other clients or used to train AI models. Only your team and your assigned Advizr staff can access your workspace.

<Callout type="info">
  Your data lives in an isolated database. No other client can see it, and it is never used outside your engagement.
</Callout>

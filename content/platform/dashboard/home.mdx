# Home

The Home screen is your daily starting point. It gives you a quick view of what needs your attention, how your engagement is progressing, and what has happened since your last visit. Think of it as your morning briefing.

## Action cards

At the top of the Home screen you will see action cards - tasks that need your attention. These might include:

- A new proposal waiting for your review
- A completed workflow with results to check
- A document that needs additional information
- A call to schedule or confirm

Each card links directly to the relevant section, so you can act on it immediately without navigating through the sidebar.

## Progress checklist

Below the action cards is your progress checklist. During onboarding, this tracks the five setup steps. After onboarding, it shifts to engagement milestones - things like "first workflow delivered," "first objective closed," or "team member added."

The checklist gives you a clear picture of where you are in the engagement and what comes next. Completed items are marked with a tick; outstanding items show what to focus on.

## Activity feed

The activity feed shows recent events across your workspace:

- Messages from your Advizr team
- Workflow completions and results
- New proposals or proposal updates
- Document processing completions
- Team changes and new member invitations

The feed is chronological, with the most recent events at the top. Click any event to jump to its full context.

<Callout type="tip">
  Check Home at the start of each day. The action cards and activity feed keep you up to date without needing to visit every section individually.
</Callout>

## Quick actions

The Home screen includes shortcuts to the most common tasks - start a new chat, book a call, upload a document, or view your latest proposal. These are the same actions available in the sidebar, but grouped here for convenience.

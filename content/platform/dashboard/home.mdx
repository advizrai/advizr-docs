# Start your day from Home

The Home screen is your daily starting point. It gives you a quick view of what needs your attention, how your engagement is progressing, and what has happened since your last visit. Think of it as your morning briefing.

/>

## Review what needs your attention

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Home from the sidebar">
    Click **Home** in the left sidebar. On mobile, tap the menu icon in the top-left corner first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work through the action cards">
    Action cards at the top of the screen are tasks waiting on you - a proposal to review, a completed workflow with results to check, a document that needs more information, or a call to schedule. Click a card to jump straight to the relevant section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your progress checklist">
    Below the action cards, the checklist shows where you are in the engagement. Completed items are marked with a checkmark; outstanding items show what to focus on next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the activity feed">
    The feed lists recent events across your workspace in chronological order, newest first. Click any event to open its full context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should now see zero unactioned cards at the top of Home and know exactly what is happening in your workspace today.

<Callout type="tip">
  Check Home at the start of each day. The action cards and activity feed keep you up to date without needing to visit every section individually.
</Callout>

## What the activity feed covers

The feed shows recent events across your workspace:

- Messages from your Advizr team
- Workflow completions and results
- New proposals or proposal updates
- Document processing completions
- Team changes and new member invitations

## Quick actions

The Home screen includes shortcuts to the most common tasks - start a new chat, book a call, upload a document, or view your latest proposal. These are the same actions available in the sidebar, but grouped here for convenience.

<Details title="Why the checklist changes after onboarding">
  During onboarding, the progress checklist tracks the five setup steps. Once onboarding is complete, it shifts to engagement milestones - things like "first workflow delivered," "first objective closed," or "team member added." This keeps the checklist relevant at every stage: it always answers "where am I, and what comes next?" rather than repeating setup tasks you have already finished.
</Details>

## If something goes wrong

- **Home looks empty.** If your account is new, action cards and feed events appear only once there is activity in your workspace. Complete the onboarding steps and check back after your kickoff call.
- **The checklist still shows onboarding steps you have finished.** Refresh the page. If a completed step still shows as outstanding, mention it in Chat so your team can update it.
- **The activity feed seems out of date.** Refresh the page - new events can take a moment to appear after a workflow or upload completes.
- **An action card links to a page you cannot act on.** Some actions, like approving proposals, require the Client Admin role. See [Roles and permissions](/docs/platform/account/roles-and-permissions).

Still stuck? Send your FDE a message in Chat with your browser, a screenshot, and when it happened.

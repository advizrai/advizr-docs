# Goals

The Goals section is where you set, track, and manage your business objectives. Every objective you create during onboarding or later appears here with its current status and progress. Goals are not just a checklist - they drive the recommendations, workflows, and learning content you receive from your Advizr team.

## Setting new goals

You can add a new goal at any time by clicking **Add Goal** at the top of the page. Write a clear, measurable objective - the same principles from the [Setting Objectives](/docs/platform/onboarding/setting-objectives) onboarding step apply here.

Each goal includes:

- **Title** - A short name for the objective
- **Description** - The full objective with context, metrics, and timeframe
- **Category** - Revenue, efficiency, adoption, or custom
- **Status** - Active, in progress, completed, or paused

## Tracking progress

Your Advizr team updates goal progress as workflows are delivered and milestones are reached. You can also update goals yourself - add notes, adjust targets, or mark milestones as complete.

The Goals page shows a summary view with all your active objectives and their current progress. Click any goal to see its full detail, including:

- Linked workflows and deliverables
- Activity history (who changed what and when)
- Notes from your FDE
- Completion percentage

<Callout type="info">
  Goals shape your engagement. When your Advizr team decides what to build next, they look at your active goals to determine priorities.
</Callout>

## Updating and closing goals

Business priorities change. You can edit any goal to update its description, targets, or category. When an objective is fully achieved, mark it as completed. Completed goals stay in your history so you can review what was accomplished.

If an objective is no longer relevant, you can pause or archive it. Paused goals do not affect recommendations or priority decisions.

## Goal categories

Goals are grouped into categories to help you see the balance of your engagement:

- **Revenue** - Goals focused on increasing income or reducing costs
- **Efficiency** - Goals focused on saving time or eliminating manual work
- **Adoption** - Goals focused on getting your team comfortable with AI tools
- **Custom** - Anything that does not fit the other categories

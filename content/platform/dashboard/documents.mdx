# Documents

The Documents section is your file library. Every document you upload - during onboarding or at any point after - appears here with its processing status. Documents feed your organisation's knowledge base, which means the AI in Chat, your workflows, and your Advizr team all draw on the files you provide.

## Uploading new documents

Click **Upload** at the top of the page to add files. You can drag and drop multiple files at once or click to browse your computer. Each file is processed individually - larger files may take a few minutes.

Supported formats:

- **Documents:** PDF, DOCX, TXT
- **Spreadsheets:** XLSX, CSV
- **Images:** PNG, JPG, JPEG

Maximum file size is 25 MB per file. If you need to upload something larger, contact your Advizr team.

<Callout type="tip">
  Keep your documents up to date. If you update an SOP or financial report, upload the new version so the AI always works with current information.
</Callout>

## How documents are processed

When you upload a file, the platform:

1. Stores the file securely in your isolated database
2. Extracts the text content and key data points
3. Adds the content to your organisation's knowledge base
4. Makes it available for Chat, workflows, and your Advizr team

A status indicator next to each file shows where it is in this process: uploading, processing, or ready.

## AI analysis

Once a document is processed, the AI can reference it in conversations and workflows. You do not need to tell the AI which document to use - it automatically finds relevant content based on your question or request. If you want to focus on a specific document, mention it by name in Chat.

<Callout type="info">
  Documents are stored in your isolated database. They are never shared with other clients or used outside your engagement.
</Callout>

## Organising your library

As your document library grows, you can:

- **Search** by file name or content keywords
- **Filter** by file type or upload date
- **Sort** by name, date, or status

## Downloading and sharing

You can download any document you have uploaded. Documents are automatically shared with your assigned Advizr team members - no separate sharing step is needed.

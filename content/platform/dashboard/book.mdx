# Book a Call

The Book section lets you schedule live conversations with your Fractional Digital Engineer (FDE). Whether you need a strategy session, a quick check-in, or hands-on training, you can find a time that works and book it directly from the platform.

## Types of sessions

Your FDE offers several types of meetings depending on what you need:

- **Strategy sessions** - Deep-dive conversations about your business objectives, priorities, and next steps. Usually 45-60 minutes.
- **Check-ins** - Regular progress reviews. Your FDE walks you through what has been delivered, what is in progress, and what is coming next. Usually 20-30 minutes.
- **Training** - Hands-on walkthroughs of new workflows, tools, or platform features. Your FDE shows you how things work and answers questions in real time.
- **Ad-hoc** - Anything that does not fit the other categories. Got a quick question or need to discuss something specific? Book an ad-hoc session.

<Callout type="info">
  Your FDE is the Advizr team member assigned to your engagement. They know your business context, objectives, and what has been built so far.
</Callout>

## How to book

The booking interface shows your FDE's available time slots in a calendar view. Times are displayed in your local timezone.

1. Pick a session type from the options at the top
2. Choose a date and time from the available slots
3. Add any notes about what you want to discuss (optional but helpful)
4. Click Confirm

You will receive a calendar invite with a video call link. A reminder email is sent 24 hours before the session.

## Rescheduling and cancelling

Need to change your appointment? Open the Book section and find your upcoming booking. Click **Reschedule** to pick a new time, or **Cancel** to free up the slot. Try to give at least 24 hours notice when cancelling.

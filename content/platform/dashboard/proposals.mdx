# Proposals

Proposals are transformation recommendations prepared by your Advizr team. When your FDE identifies an opportunity to automate a process, build a new workflow, or improve an existing system, they create a proposal for your review. The Proposals section is where you read the details, leave feedback, and approve or request changes.

## What a proposal contains

Each proposal typically includes:

- **Summary** - A brief description of what is being recommended and why
- **Scope** - What will be built, changed, or automated
- **Timeline** - How long the work will take and key milestones
- **Expected outcomes** - What you can expect to gain (time saved, costs reduced, processes improved)
- **Connected goals** - Which of your business objectives this proposal supports

Proposals are written in plain language. If anything is unclear, leave a comment or ask in Chat.

## Proposal lifecycle

Proposals move through a set of stages:

1. **Draft** - Your FDE is still working on the details. You may see drafts if your FDE shares early for feedback.
2. **Review** - The proposal is ready for you to read and respond to.
3. **Approved** - You have approved the work. Your FDE begins building.
4. **In Progress** - The work is underway. You can track progress from the Goals and Runs sections.
5. **Completed** - The work is done and delivered. Results are available in your dashboard.

## Responding to proposals

When a proposal is in Review status, you can:

- **Approve** - Confirm that you want the work to proceed as described
- **Request changes** - Ask your FDE to adjust the scope, timeline, or approach
- **Comment** - Leave questions or notes on specific parts of the proposal

<Callout type="info">
  Only users with the Client Admin role can approve proposals. Client Viewers can read proposals and leave comments but cannot approve them. See [Roles and Permissions](/docs/platform/account/roles-and-permissions) for details.
</Callout>

## Finding past proposals

All proposals - active and completed - are listed on the Proposals page. Use the status filter to narrow the view. Completed proposals include a summary of what was delivered and the outcomes achieved, which is useful for tracking the value of your engagement over time.

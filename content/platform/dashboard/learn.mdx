# Build your skills in Learn

The Learn section gives you access to educational content from the Advizr Academy - courses, guides, and case studies organized by topic and tailored to where you are in your engagement. The goal is to help you and your team understand the AI tools being built for your business, so you get the most out of them.

## Start a course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Learn from the sidebar">
    Click **Learn** in the left sidebar. On mobile, tap the menu icon in the top-left corner first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a course, guide, or case study">
    The library highlights content recommended for your current phase. Click any item to open it. If you are looking for something specific, browse by topic instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work through the modules">
    Each course has multiple modules you can complete at your own pace. Your progress is saved automatically as you go.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Come back any time">
    When you return to Learn, pick up where you left off - finished modules are marked complete, and your place in each course is preserved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should now see the course listed with your completion progress, showing which modules are finished, in progress, and still ahead.

<Callout type="tip">
  If you are new to working with AI in a business context, start with the Foundations content. It covers the core concepts in about 30 minutes.
</Callout>

## What is in the library

The Learn section includes several types of content:

- **Courses** - Structured lessons on specific topics. Each course has multiple modules that you can complete at your own pace.
- **Guides** - Shorter, practical documents that explain how to do a specific thing - for example, how to write good objectives or get the most from Chat.
- **Case studies** - Real examples of how businesses similar to yours have used AI to solve problems and save time.

Content is updated regularly as new material is published.

## Content matched to your phase

Your engagement with Advizr moves through phases - from initial setup and onboarding through to advanced automation and scaling. The Learn section highlights content that matches your current phase: foundational material early on, then more advanced topics like interpreting analytics, managing team adoption, and optimizing workflows as your engagement progresses.

<Details title="Why content is matched to your phase">
  Showing everything at once buries the material you actually need this week. Phase-based recommendations surface the content that maps to what your Advizr team is building right now - so when a new workflow lands, the lesson that explains how to use it well is already at the top of your library. You can always browse beyond the recommendations; nothing is locked.
</Details>

## Learn with your team

If you have team members on the platform, they can access the same Learn content from their own dashboard. Progress is tracked individually, so each person can work through material at their own pace.

## If something goes wrong

- **Your progress did not save.** Progress saves automatically, but only while you are online. Check your connection, refresh the page, and confirm you are signed in to the same account.
- **The recommendations feel too basic or too advanced.** Recommendations follow your engagement phase, but you are free to browse the full library. If the phase itself looks wrong, mention it in Chat.
- **A course your FDE mentioned is not in your library.** New material is published on a rolling basis and some content is phase-specific. Ask in Chat and your team can point you to it or tell you when it lands.

Still stuck? Send your FDE a message in Chat with your browser, a screenshot, and when it happened.

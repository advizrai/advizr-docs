# Learn

The Learn section gives you access to educational content from the Advizr Academy. Courses, guides, and case studies are organised by topic and tailored to where you are in your engagement. The goal is to help you and your team understand the AI tools being built for your business, so you can get the most out of them.

## What is in the library

The Learn section includes several types of content:

- **Courses** - Structured lessons on specific topics like "Understanding AI Automation" or "Working with Data." Each course has multiple modules that you can complete at your own pace.
- **Guides** - Shorter, practical documents that explain how to do a specific thing - for example, "How to write good objectives" or "Getting the most from Chat."
- **Case studies** - Real examples of how businesses similar to yours have used AI to solve problems and save time.

Content is updated regularly as new material is published.

## Phase-based learning

Your engagement with Advizr moves through phases - from initial setup and onboarding through to advanced automation and scaling. The Learn section highlights content that matches your current phase.

Early in your engagement, you will see foundational content about what AI can do and how to work with the platform. As your engagement progresses, the recommendations shift toward more advanced topics like interpreting analytics, managing team adoption, and optimising workflows.

<Callout type="tip">
  Start with the Foundations content if you are new to working with AI in a business context. It covers the core concepts in about 30 minutes.
</Callout>

## Tracking your progress

Each course tracks your completion progress. You can see which modules you have finished, which are in progress, and which are still ahead. Pick up where you left off at any time - your progress is saved automatically.

## Sharing with your team

If you have team members on the platform, they can access the same Learn content from their own dashboard. Progress is tracked individually, so each person can work through material at their own pace.

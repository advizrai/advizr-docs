# Help

The Help section is where you go when you need answers or support. It brings together common questions, documentation links, and direct contact options in one place. For most questions, start with Chat - it is the fastest way to get a response.

## Getting support

There are three ways to get help, listed from fastest to most thorough:

### 1. Ask in Chat

Open the [Chat](/docs/platform/dashboard/chat) section and type your question. The AI has access to your business context, documents, and platform documentation. For most questions - "How do I upload a document?", "What is the status of my latest workflow?", "How do I add a team member?" - Chat gives you an immediate answer.

### 2. Browse the FAQ

The Help page includes answers to the most common questions. Topics are grouped by category - account, billing, platform features, and troubleshooting. Click any question to expand the answer.

For detailed FAQ content, see:
- [General FAQ](/docs/platform/faq/general)
- [Troubleshooting](/docs/platform/faq/troubleshooting)
- [Data and Privacy](/docs/platform/faq/data-and-privacy)

### 3. Book a call

If you need to talk things through, [book a call](/docs/platform/dashboard/book) with your FDE. Strategy questions, complex issues, and anything that benefits from a conversation are best handled live.

<Callout type="tip">
  Start with Chat for the fastest answer. If Chat cannot resolve your question, it will suggest booking a call with your FDE.
</Callout>

## Contact options

If you need to reach the Advizr team outside the platform:

- **Email** - Send a message to your FDE directly. Their email is listed in the Book section.
- **Chat** - Start a new thread with your question.
- **Book a call** - Schedule a dedicated session.

## Response time expectations

Chat responses are typically instant for questions the AI can answer directly. For questions that need human input from your FDE, expect a response within one business day. Urgent issues are flagged and prioritised.

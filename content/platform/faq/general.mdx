# General FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about the Advizr platform. If you do not find what you are looking for here, ask in [Chat](/docs/platform/dashboard/chat) or [book a call](/docs/platform/dashboard/book) with your FDE.

<Details title="What is Advizr?">
  Advizr is an AI transformation partner for small and medium businesses. We pair you with a dedicated Fractional Digital Engineer (FDE) who builds custom AI workflows for your business, trains your team, and manages the technology so you can focus on running your company. The platform at advizrclients.com is where you manage the engagement - set goals, upload documents, chat with AI, and review proposals.
</Details>

<Details title="Who is the platform for?">
  The platform is designed for business owners and their teams. You do not need technical skills to use it. The interface is built around plain-language Chat, clear dashboards, and guided workflows. Whether you are a sole founder or have a team of 50, the platform adapts to your role and needs.
</Details>

<Details title="How do I get started?">
  After signing up, you will receive an email invitation. Click the link to set your password, then log in at advizrclients.com. A five-step onboarding wizard guides you through setting objectives, uploading documents, booking your kickoff call, and using Chat for the first time. The whole process takes about 15 minutes. See [Getting Started](/docs/platform/getting-started) for a full walkthrough.
</Details>

<Details title="What can I do in the platform?">
  You can chat with AI that knows your business context, set and track business objectives, upload documents for analysis, review transformation proposals from your Advizr team, access learning content, book calls with your FDE, and monitor automated workflows. The platform is your central hub for everything related to your Advizr engagement.
</Details>

<Details title="What is a Fractional Digital Engineer?">
  Your FDE is the Advizr team member assigned to your engagement. They are a real person - not a bot - who builds your workflows, configures your AI tools, and meets with you regularly to review progress. "Fractional" means they work with you on a part-time basis as part of your subscription, rather than as a full-time hire.
</Details>

<Details title="How often should I log in?">
  Most clients check in a few times per week. The Home screen shows action cards and recent activity, so a quick daily check keeps you up to date. If you prefer less frequent visits, enable [email digests](/docs/platform/features/notifications) to get summaries delivered to your inbox.
</Details>

<Details title="Can my team use the platform?">
  Yes. You can invite team members from [Team Management](/docs/platform/features/team-management). Each person gets their own login and a role that controls what they can see and do. Roles include Client Admin (full access), Client Viewer (read-only), and Client Consumer (simplified, chat-focused experience).
</Details>

<Details title="Is there a long-term contract?">
  No. Advizr subscriptions are month-to-month. You can cancel at any time with no early termination fees. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. See [Billing](/docs/platform/account/billing) for details.
</Details>

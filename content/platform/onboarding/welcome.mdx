# The Onboarding Wizard

When you first log in, a five-step onboarding wizard walks you through setting up your workspace. The wizard collects the information your Advizr team needs to tailor your experience - your objectives, key documents, and scheduling preferences. It takes about 15 minutes to complete, and you can pause and return to it at any time.

## The five steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome and account setup">
    Confirm your name, company details, and contact information. This is also where you set your profile photo and verify your email address. The information you provide here appears throughout the platform and helps your Advizr team understand your organisation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your first objective">
    Define what you want to achieve with AI. A clear objective gives your Advizr team direction and shapes the recommendations, workflows, and learning content you receive. You can always add more objectives later from the Goals section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload key documents">
    Share business documents that give the platform context about your operations. Think SOPs, org charts, process maps, financial summaries, or marketing materials. The AI uses these to give you more relevant answers and recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book your kickoff call">
    Pick a time to meet your Fractional Digital Engineer (FDE). The kickoff call is where you review your objectives, discuss your documents, and agree on a plan for the first month. You will receive a calendar invite with a video link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting with your AI">
    Send your first message in Chat. Try asking a question about your business, requesting an analysis of a document you uploaded, or asking what the platform can do for you. This step shows you how Chat works before you leave the wizard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to prepare before you start

You will get through the wizard faster if you have a few things ready:

- A clear idea of one business goal you want AI to help with
- Two or three key documents about your business (PDF, DOCX, or similar)
- Your calendar open so you can pick a time for your kickoff call

<Callout type="tip">
  You do not need to upload every document right away. Start with the most important ones and add more later from the Documents section.
</Callout>

## Resuming the wizard

If you close the browser or navigate away, the wizard picks up where you left off the next time you log in. A progress checklist on your Home screen shows which steps you have completed and which are still outstanding.

# Book your first call

Step four of the onboarding wizard is scheduling your kickoff call. This is your first live conversation with your Fractional Digital Engineer (FDE) — the Advizr team member assigned to your engagement. The call sets the direction for your first month and makes sure your objectives and documents are properly understood.

## What happens on the kickoff call

The kickoff call is typically 30-45 minutes. Your FDE will:

- Review the objectives you set during onboarding
- Discuss the documents you uploaded and ask clarifying questions
- Explain what will be built in your first month
- Walk you through the platform features you will use most
- Answer any questions you have about the process

You do not need to prepare a presentation or formal brief. Come with your objectives in mind and any questions about how AI can help your business.

<Details title="What is an FDE?">
  Your Fractional Digital Engineer is a dedicated Advizr team member who manages your engagement. They build your workflows, configure your AI tools, and meet with you regularly to review progress and adjust the plan.
</Details>

## Book the call

<Steps>
  <Step title="View available times">
    The wizard shows a calendar with your FDE's available time slots. Times are displayed in your local timezone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a slot">
    Click on a time that works for you. Morning and afternoon slots are usually available within the next few business days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the booking">
    Review the date, time, and duration, then click **Confirm** to lock in the appointment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should now see a booking confirmation in the wizard and a calendar invite with a video call link in your email inbox.

## After the call

After your kickoff call, your FDE will send you a summary of what was discussed and a list of action items. These typically include:

- Specific workflows that will be built in the first sprint
- Any additional documents they need from you
- A timeline for your first deliverables
- The date for your next check-in call

You can always rebook or reschedule from the [Book a Call](/docs/platform/dashboard/book) section of your dashboard.

## If something goes wrong

- **No time slots work for you.** Slots refresh as your FDE's calendar changes — check back the next business day, or send a message in Chat with the times you can do.
- **The calendar invite never arrived.** Check your spam folder and confirm the email address on your profile is correct. Your booking still exists — the time is shown in the Book a Call section.
- **You need to reschedule.** Open [Book a Call](/docs/platform/dashboard/book) and pick a new time. Rescheduling before the call costs you nothing.
- **The video link does not work at call time.** Reply to the calendar invite or message your FDE in Chat — they will send a fresh link.

Still stuck? Send your FDE a message in Chat with your browser, a screenshot, and when it happened.
